LBS INSTITUTE OF TECHNOLOGY FOR WOMEN

LBS Institute of Technology for Women (LBSITW) is a self-financing engineering college located in Thiruvananthapuram,
Kerala. It was established in 2001 and is the only engineering college for women in the government sector in the state
of Kerala.013 LBSITW offers B.Tech and M.Tech degree programs in various branches of Engineering.The college is affiliated
to the University of Kerala and is managed by the LBS Centre for Science and Technology,Trivandrum. The mission of the
college is to make the institution a center of excellence that molds women engineers of quality in the latest frontiers of
Science and Technology so as to play a vital role in the socio-economic progress of the nation.
LBSITW is affiliated with the APJ Abdul Kalam Technological University and is approved by the AICTE.

About US

College Board is a mission-driven not-for-profit organization that connects students to college success and opportunity.
Founded in 1900, College Board was created to expand access to higher education. Today, the membership association is made
up of over 6,000 of the world’s leading educational institutions and is dedicated to promoting excellence and equity in education.
Each year, College Board helps more than seven million students prepare for a successful transition to college through programs
and services in college readiness and college success—including the SAT, the Advanced Placement Program, and BigFuture.
The organization also serves the education community through research and advocacy on behalf of students, educators, and schools.
